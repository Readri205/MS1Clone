MTB & Gravel Bike Tours

Catalonia & Pyrenees

Bike Touring

Enjoy a variety of bike tours around the region, MTB or Gravel Bike. Routes are primarily off-road cycle tracks with no traffic. Routes are flat or mountainous to suit. Accomodation varies from camping beneath the stars to 5* Hotel accomodation with the stars!

Landscape

The region is packed with stunning geography from the magestic coastal region to the mountainous Eastern Pyrenees. History abounds with Roman and Napoleanic artifacts, and quaint Catalan villages. Dahli was a local and his art influences the area!

Food & Culture

The area is rich with Catalan culture on both sides of the border. It is a proud region that maintains its culture and language but perhaps more so its culinary delights. The region is rich in local produce and cuisines that will suit all tastes!

About

RMC Tours is a small company organising bike tours in the Catalonia area of Spain and France. Our team is passionate about biking and discovering all the historical, geographical and culinary treasures in the area.
